What Does My Status Mean?

  • Currently in Progress: This status means that we have not viewed your appointment application as of yet; do not worry, we are making sure to get back to you as soon as possible. No appointment date will be shown yet as we have not discussed a time. In the rare event that it takes us longer than a week to get back to you, please contact us so we can work towards fixing the issue.
  • Confirmed: This status means that you and one of our associates has went over the submitted application and come to an agreement on a time and date for the appointment. Once your appointment has been confirmed, you should be able to see the time and date of the appointment below the status.
  • Under Repair: This status means that your car is now at our workshop being fixed. You will bring your car (or a towing service will) on the scheduled appointment date. We prioritize the quality of the repair over the quickness, so please give us adequate time to ensure that your car is in good condition.
  • Completed: This status means that your car has been fully repaired and is ready to go. When the 'Completed' status is shown, you can come by and take your car after you pay the required amount.
