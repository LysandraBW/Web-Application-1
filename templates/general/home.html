Appointments

It's just our way of being efficient.

To begin the process of having your car repaired by us, you must schedule an appointment; this is just a measure taken to ensure a quick and easy experience for our beloved customers. You can schedule an appointment on our website or by calling us or by meeting us in person. Due to the ongoing pandemic, we recommend that you fill out the online form.
After you submit a form, regardless of how you did, we will contact you as soon as possible to discuss the date of the appointment along with other specifics and questions you may have. You can also view the information regarding your appointment on our website; you'll be able to see the status of your car's repairment and the date and time of your appointment. If you have anymore questions about how appointments work, feel free to check out our FAQ or contact us via email or phone.

Payment

We're understanding people.

After we finish repairing your car, you will be able to pay for the service in-person or online. If you choose to pay in-person, we take cash or credit; if the latter, it's only possible for us to take credit. Your financial information is safe with us, we work with the most secure brands to ensure that your information stays with you and does not end up in the hands of malicious people. Unfortunately, we do not allow our customers to pay in installments, this is just taken as a security measure. However, it's unlikely that you will need installments because of our low prices.
After you've paid for the service, we will gladly return to you your car key and you will be a happy person driving a happy car, happy merrily after!
